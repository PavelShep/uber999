Commission 8% + BONUSES
We have a minimum commission and we pay bonuses for each trip, some of our competitors leave bonuses to themselves. We work honestly and drivers earn more with us!
High income with guaranteed payments
We pay wages to the driver's personal bank card! You do not need to formalize an LLC, IE, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and bank account maintenance.
Work at convenient times, no schedules
You simply click the get on line button to start working, when you decide to finish, you click get off the line. You can only work part-time in the morning, evening, weekends or holidays!
